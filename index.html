Intro

EigenAge is a longevity research company based in Ecuador, focused on understanding and modifying the biological processes of aging through systems science and applied diagnostics.

Our work spans multiple domains, including:

  • Physiological complexity analysis (e.g., heart rate variability, EEG entropy, biomarker time series)
  • Mathematical modeling of aging trajectories using differential systems and information theory
  • Development of non-invasive diagnostics for tracking biological age and healthspan
  • Biomarker research, including mitochondrial health, inflammation, and stem cell dynamics
  • Personalized longevity protocols, informed by real-time data and longitudinal trends

We combine rigorous scientific inquiry with accessible tools to empower both researchers and individuals in the pursuit of extended, high-quality life.

Our Research Areas

  • Signal Complexity Analysis: Real-time assessment of physiological signals using entropy, fractals, and nonlinear models.
  • Mathematical Aging Models: Dynamical system frameworks for modeling biological aging trajectories.
  • Biomarker Tracking: Development of open-source tools for longitudinal analysis of inflammation, mitochondrial health, and immune shifts.
  • Field Diagnostics: Prototyping low-friction, non-invasive tools for decentralized longevity tracking.

About

EigenAge is a longevity research company based in Ecuador, dedicated to advancing the science of aging through quantitative modeling, biomarker innovation, and non-invasive diagnostics. We view aging as a dynamic and modifiable system — one that can be understood, tracked, and eventually influenced through the right combination of data, mathematics, and biology.

Our team brings together expertise in mathematics, computational biology, molecular diagnostics, and systems engineering. We focus on extracting meaningful patterns from complex physiological signals and developing field-ready tools for biological age tracking, resilience scoring, and early dysfunction detection.

EigenAge operates with a clear ethos: our tools and insights must be open-source, low-cost, scalable, and accessible. We believe that longevity science should not be confined to elite labs or expensive platforms—it should empower individuals, clinicians, and communities worldwide to take control of their healthspan using transparent and democratized technologies.

We are committed to bridging the gap between frontier aging research and actionable health technologies—transforming complexity into clarity, and science into tools anyone can use.
