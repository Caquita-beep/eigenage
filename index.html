Intro

EigenAge is a longevity research company based in Ecuador, focused on understanding and modifying the biological processes of aging through systems science and applied diagnostics.

Our work spans multiple domains, including:

  • Physiological complexity analysis (e.g., heart rate variability, EEG entropy, biomarker time series)
  • Mathematical modeling of aging trajectories using differential systems and information theory
  • Development of non-invasive diagnostics for tracking biological age and healthspan
  • Biomarker research, including mitochondrial health, inflammation, and stem cell dynamics
  • Personalized longevity protocols, informed by real-time data and longitudinal trends

We combine rigorous scientific inquiry with accessible tools to empower both researchers and individuals in the pursuit of extended, high-quality life.
